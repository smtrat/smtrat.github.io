Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class smtrat::pmatrix< T >

A map that maps from a pair of std::size_t integers to T.

Initially, the idea for that was a simple matrix; this, however, turned out to be to expensive memory-wise, thus, a hash-based map is used instead (this makes use of the sparseness of the entries).

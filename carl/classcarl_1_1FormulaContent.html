Gives the string representation of this formula content.

Parameters
_withActivityA flag which indicates whether to add the formula's activity to the result.
_resolveUnequalA switch which indicates how to represent the relation symbol for unequal. (for further description see documentation of Constraint::toString( .. ))
_initThe initial string of every row of the result.
_onelineA flag indicating whether the formula shall be printed on one line.
_infixA flag indicating whether to print the formula in infix or prefix notation.
_friendlyNamesA flag that indicates whether to print the variables with their internal representation (false) or with their dedicated names.
Returns
The resulting string representation of this formula.

Detailed Description

template<typename NodeType, NodeType *NodeType::* PrevPtr, NodeType *NodeType::* NextPtr>
class smtrat::linked_list< NodeType, PrevPtr, NextPtr >

An intrusive linked list pseudo-container that does not take ownership of its contents.

Note: this somewhat resembles a standard container, however it is NOT a standard-compliant container (misses size(), does not really contain nodes, rather points to them). However, the for(... : ...) syntax works and the iterators are standard-compliant.
